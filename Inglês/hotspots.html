Our trip to France

Here are the places we will visit


Torre Eiffel

  • Rising gracefully above the Parisian skyline, the Eiffel Tower stands as an enduring symbol of elegance, romance, and the indomitable spirit of Paris. This iconic landmark, located on the Champ de Mars, is not only a testament to architectural brilliance but also a must-visit destination for anyone exploring the wonders of France.



The Louvre Museum

  • The Louvre Museum, located in the heart of Paris, France, is one of the most iconic and revered cultural landmarks in the world. With its rich history, magnificent architecture, and extensive art collection, it stands as a testament to human creativity and ingenuity.



Catacombs of Paris

  • Deep beneath the bustling streets of Paris lies a mysterious and haunting underworld known as the Catacombs. This underground labyrinth, hidden from the eyes of passersby, holds a captivating history and offers a unique and unforgettable experience for intrepid explorers.



Picasso Museum

  • The Picasso Museum, located in the picturesque city of Paris, France, is a cultural gem that pays homage to the artistic genius of Pablo Picasso. Housed in the splendid Hôtel Salé, a stunning 17th-century mansion in the historic Marais district, the museum offers a captivating exploration of Picasso's life and artistic evolution.



Galeries Lafayette Haussmann

  • Located in the heart of Paris, the Galeries Lafayette Haussmann is a world-renowned shopping destination that exudes elegance, luxury, and style. With its rich history and stunning architecture, this iconic department store offers visitors a truly unforgettable shopping experience.



Monet garden

  • Tucked away in the picturesque region of Normandy, France, the Government: Monet's Garden is a breathtaking haven of natural beauty and artistic inspiration. This enchanting tourist attraction pays tribute to the renowned Impressionist painter Claude Monet, who transformed the landscape into a living work of art.



Palace of Versalhes

  • The Palace of Versailles, also known as the "Château de Versailles," is an iconic French landmark that epitomizes opulence, grandeur, and the power of monarchy. Located just outside of Paris, this magnificent palace has a rich history and serves as a testament to the artistic and architectural achievements of the French monarchy.



Caen Memorial Museum

  • Located in the historic city of Caen, France, the Memorial Museum is a powerful and thought-provoking tribute to the events of World War II and the quest for peace. This exceptional museum provides visitors with a comprehensive understanding of the war, its impact on humanity, and the importance of fostering peace in our world.
